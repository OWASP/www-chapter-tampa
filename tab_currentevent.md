**Description:** 
Stop Chasing Ghosts and ClickBait

Every week a new spooky headline makes the rounds, whether it is a zero-day, a nation-state threat, or the latest malware campaign with a catchy name. The result is that organizations hit the panic button, leadership loses sleep, and teams waste time chasing ghosts in the wire that may never cross their path. In this Ghostbusters themed talk, we will unpack why these fear cycles keep haunting security teams, how panic spreads faster than phishing emails, and what it really takes to tell the difference between shadows and real specters. If you are tired of ghost stories running your threat response, it is time to refine your approach and rethink your threat hunting methodologies
